Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what GutWell collects and how we handle it.

1. Data We Collect

GutWell collects the data you voluntarily enter: check-in logs (stool type, bloating, pain, energy, mood, water intake), food logs (meal names, meal types, meal photos you choose to analyze), symptom logs (type, severity), supplement notes, onboarding answers (your gut concerns and goals), and basic account information (email, display name). If you use voice input, your speech is converted to text on your device or by your phone’s operating system; we store only the resulting text. If you grant location access for local food suggestions, your approximate location is used during that analysis.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide the GutWell service — displaying your health trends, calculating your gut health score, detecting food–symptom patterns, personalizing AI meal analysis, and sending reminders you configure. We never sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising or cross-app tracking.

3. Service Providers We Share Data With

To run GutWell we use a small number of processors, each bound by their own privacy commitments: • Supabase (hosted on AWS, EU region) — stores your account and all logs described above. • Google Gemini API — when you analyze a meal photo, the photo plus the context you provide (symptoms, conditions, supplements, and — if enabled — approximate location) is sent to Google’s AI service to generate your analysis. Google does not use this data to train models per its API terms. • PostHog (analytics) — anonymous-style usage events (e.g. “check-in logged”) linked to a random user ID. We do not send your health values, name, or email to analytics. • Sentry (crash reporting) — technical crash data, not linked to your identity. • Apple / RevenueCat — only if you make a purchase, to process and validate it. We do not sell or share your personal health data with any other third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS) with encryption at rest and in transit. Row-level security ensures only you can access your data. Authentication tokens are stored in your device’s secure storage. AI requests are processed by our server — API keys never live in the app.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can export your data at any time from Settings or Profile. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly in the app: Profile → Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, correct, or delete your personal data (GDPR Articles 15–20 where applicable). You can exercise these rights through the app settings or by contacting us. You may also withdraw location, camera, microphone, or notification permissions at any time in your device Settings.

7. Contact

GutWell is operated by Parallel Labs Pte. Ltd. For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@theparallellab.com.
